# ✈️ Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis
 
> *What keeps a passenger coming back — and what drives them away?*
 
In an industry where every seat counts, understanding the passenger experience is not just a priority — it is a necessity. This project dives deep into the factors that shape how passengers feel about their journey, from the moment they book online to the second they land.
 
---
 
## Problem Statement
 
££ Airline has been experiencing a significant decline in its customer base, which poses a serious threat to its market competitiveness and long-term revenue. Preliminary observations suggest that this attrition may be driven by deficiencies across multiple service dimensions, including in-flight and digital services (e.g., Wi-Fi connectivity and online booking experience), as well as inconsistencies in service quality across both **Business** and **Economy** travel classes. These shortcomings are likely contributing to deteriorating levels of customer satisfaction.
